SYNOPSIS あらすじ
"Is this all just a dream?”, She thought to herself in silence. Her starry gaze locked upon the luminous lights, Eyes sparkling like the moon shining over the obsidian ocean. Picking up her leftovers, she sets off as night falls.- The identity and descent of the “Dream Machines” remains unknown. With recurring memories of childhood reminiscences and pop culture, It fuels their ability to live prosperously amongst themselves. SORA is the only human living amongst the 10,000 Dream Machines.
DREAM MACHINES
The Dream Machines are 10,000 unique Gen 0 avatars.
There are over 200 assets paired with 6 traits, randomly generated on the blockchain.
The permutations are endless, resulting in limitless possibilities ranked by their rarity.
Each and every detail has been painstakingly hand illustrated by our talented artists.
Immerse yourself in Sora’s Dreamworld to unravel what lies ahead in this superlunary world.
The cost to mint a Dream Machine is 0.08 ETH

ABOUT

Profile Pictures (PFP) have become a way of how people represent themselves to be over the internet in this new paradigm. Infusing Lo-Fi aesthetics to our work with a mixture of pop culture and all things fun is the perfect concoction for expressing good vibes only.

Recreating precious memories crafted like a time capsule into our project is how we perceive Sora's Dreamworld to be. A home to digital nomads, PFPs signing off the blockchain with these signatory aesthetics that live on chain forever. You will always have a home, a sense of belonging and an identity by being a part of our wholesome community.

We strongly believe in a cohesive community and are always open to unique suggestions and creative ideas. The roadmap will be heavily influenced by our decisions together with pre determined check marks fulfilled along the way. We intend to be highly community driven and motivated throughout the course of execution as we believe in fair opportunities for creatives in the space. We would most definitely reward good deeds and supportiveness of each other along the way. Kindness and positivity goes a long way and will not go unnoticed.
